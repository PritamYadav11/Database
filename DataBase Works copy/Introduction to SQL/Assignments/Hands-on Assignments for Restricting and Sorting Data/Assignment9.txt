Q. The HR department needs a report that displays the last name and hire date for all employees who were hired in 1981.

Answer :-

SELECT ENAME, HIREDATE
FROM EMP
WHERE TO_CHAR(HIREDATE, 'YYYY') = '1981';

also correct:

SELECT ENAME, HIREDATE
FROM EMP
WHERE HIREDATE BETWEEN TO_DATE('01-JAN-1981','DD-MON-YYYY')
                  AND TO_DATE('31-DEC-1981','DD-MON-YYYY');
