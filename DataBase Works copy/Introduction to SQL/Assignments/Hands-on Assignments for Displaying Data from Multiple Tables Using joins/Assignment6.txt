Q. The HR department needs a report on job grades and salaries. First show the structure of the SALGRADE table. Then display the employee name, job, department name, salary, and grade for all employees.

Answer :-

DESC SALGRADE;

SELECT E.ENAME,
       E.JOB,
       D.DNAME,
       E.SAL,
       S.GRADE
FROM EMP E, DEPT D, SALGRADE S
WHERE E.DEPTNO = D.DEPTNO
  AND E.SAL BETWEEN S.LOSAL AND S.HISAL;
