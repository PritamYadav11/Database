Q. The HR department needs to find the names and hire dates for all employees who were hired before their managers, along with their managers' names and hire dates.

Answer :-

SELECT E.ENAME,
       E.HIREDATE,
       M.ENAME AS MANAGER,
       M.HIREDATE
FROM EMP E
LEFT OUTER JOIN EMP M
ON E.MGR = M.EMPNO
WHERE E.HIREDATE < M.HIREDATE;
