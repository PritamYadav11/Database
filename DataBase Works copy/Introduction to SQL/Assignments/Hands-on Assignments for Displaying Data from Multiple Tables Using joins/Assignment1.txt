Q. Write a query for the HR department to produce the addresses of all the departments. Use the EMP and DEPT tables. Show the EMPNO, ENAME, SAL, DNAME and LOC in the output. Use a NATURAL JOIN to produce the results.

Answer :-

SELECT EMPNO, ENAME, SAL, DNAME, LOC
FROM EMP
NATURAL JOIN DEPT;
