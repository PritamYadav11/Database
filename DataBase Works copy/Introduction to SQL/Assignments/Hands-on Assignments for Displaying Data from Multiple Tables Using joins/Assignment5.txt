Q. Modify the previous query to display all employees including KING, who has no manager. Order the results by the employee number.

Answer :-

SELECT E.ENAME AS "Employee",
       E.EMPNO AS "Emp#",
       M.ENAME AS "Manager",
       M.EMPNO AS "Mgr#"
FROM EMP E
LEFT OUTER JOIN EMP M
ON E.MGR = M.EMPNO
ORDER BY E.EMPNO;
